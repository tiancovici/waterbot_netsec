Dress Code

Mandatory for all students:

  • White GI for White, Yellow and Orange belts
  • Black or White Traditional GI for Purple belts and above
  • Masters of Karate patch on front left side of GI
  • Flag patch on the right shoulder
  • Martial arts sneakers (no exceptions)
  • Groin protector
  • Ornamental patches must be approved by Instructor
  • NO outside style patches allowed
  • NO personal lettering of any kind on uniform
  • Full uniform must be worn to all Tests, Clinics and Tournaments

Optional:

  • Black or White Kempo GI for Green belts and above
  • Summer Uniform (Memorial - Labor Day) Masters TShirt may be subsituited for GI top.

    Rules of the School

    • EFFORT - putting forth an exertion of strength or power, physically or mentally
    • ETIQUETTE - the conventional code of good manners that governs behavior in society; professional and business decorum
    • CHARACTER - the total sum of qualities making up an individual; moral excellence
    • SINCERITY - honesty of mind or intentions; truthfullness
    • SELF-CONTROL - control over ones own impulses, acts, or emotions

    Rules of Conduct

    • Remove shoes and line them up neatly BEFORE entering dojo; when leaving replace shoes outside dojo.
    • Bow whenever entering or leaving main dojo to show respect
    • Always walk behind a class in progress; never walk in front of the instructor while class is in progress.
    • If you arrive before your class begins, be seated quietly behind the class in progress and wait for your class to begin.
    • All students are expected to be on time for their scheduled class. Chronic tardiness is disrespectful and will not be tolerated. (Parents: please adhere to this policy as best as you can. We realize emergencies do occur.)
    • Absolutely NO sparring without Black Belt supervision. Illegal target areas are the face, groin, legs and back. Legal target areas are the area between the neck and belt, only when protected.
    • Protective gear required for sparring is: Authorized Safe-T-Kick, chops, instep, headgear, mouth guard, and groin protection
    • Please knock on the dressing room and office doors before entering.
    • All students are required to know the rules of the school Honor Code and The 5 Animals.
